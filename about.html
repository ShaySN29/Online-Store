Our Story

Drinking a hot cup of tea can be one of the most effective ways to lower stress. I was always an anxious person, however I was most relaxed and calm when drinking a cup of chai with my grandmother. She would mix her own whole spices and grind them into a powder and the smell would waft into the air nd everything would start to calm


After losing my grandmother in 2020, I struggled to find that calm again until I made myself a cup of her chai recipe. I then decided that I would share it with the world and that is how Chai There! was born.


How to make a Chai There! latte like my grandmother

Ingredients:

  1. 2 TBS Chai There! latte powder
  2. 1 cup of water
  3. Sweetner (my grandmother preferred condensed milk)
  4. Milk (coconut or non-dairy could be used too)

Boil the water on a stovetop and add the Chai There! powder once boiled. Mix unil all the powder is dissolved and then add your sweetener and finally the milk. Let the chai latte simmer for 2min and pour into a cup and enjoy...
